I am a big fan of music. Apart from classical music and other types of music I like the most. To be exact, if I like the rhythm of the song, it is very likely that I will like it. Speaking about my favorite music I have to highlight two. Trap/rap and reggaeton. On the trap/rap side we have Eladio Carrion. On the other hand, in reggaeton my favorite referent is Bad bunny. But if I had to choose one genre, it would be trap.



Like I said before, my favorite music is rap/trap. Apart from the rhythm that appears in each song of this genre, I have to say that in this case what I love about this genre is the lyrics. The lyrics of the songs literally touch my heart. In fact I have acquired the habit of listening to this genre of music every time I study or want to distract myself. Eladio Carrión is the singer who most conveys sensations to me with his lyrics. I guess it's because I identify with them.
